You have a heavy object impacting the water in the barrel with great force, forcing a large volume of water out through the top and the pipes on the side. This created an area with lower pressure than the air outside it, and so it crumpled inwards. Same kind of effect you get when you vacuum all the air out of a tin can or balloon.
