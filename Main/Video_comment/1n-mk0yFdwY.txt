Awesome build! If i could give some friendly advice, you may want to rethink the hard mount on the lift points of the 3 point attachment. If you switched your design around to have a pivot point on a solid shaft attached to your current mount..... you should be able to use 3 point lift arms for mounting. This would allow you to easily remove the whole setup just like it was on a tractor. BUT... the more important part is that WHEN, not if..... you manage to catch a rock, or a steeper incline, the arms will raise up and save the whole thing from being destroyed by inertia meeting an immovable object. Keep in mind 3 points on tractors can only lift. They cannot exert downward force. Because of this, they are naturally floating. So, if you run across a large rock or root or something that would otherwise stop you dead.... the 3 point arms just lift up.
