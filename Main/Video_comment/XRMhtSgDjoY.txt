Vital Wheat Gluten like pure protein (like 80%) and is meant to be added to normal bread flour (around 12-14%) to increase it's protein content, and adds chewiness to the bagel. This is because protein in flour helps create strong gluten strands (hence the name). So as you can imagine, making bagel dough out of only vital wheat gluten will leave you with a dough of gluten strands that are tough as nails.   Vital Wheat Gluten is great for bagels! But like, you wanna use around 1-2 tablespoons with 500g of bread flour.
