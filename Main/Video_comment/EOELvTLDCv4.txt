Peace Walker is a different story, it was a very ugly game. Birth By Sleep did as well by fixing camera controls but Peace Walker plays like a whole new game. It used this weird control setup that used the face buttons for the camera but it was completely overhauled on PS3 to closer resemble the controls of MGS4 which was also on PS3.
