I have a work around for the allays item water fountain:  Items list:  x2 note blocks x4 comparators x2 lecterns x2 written books with 15 pages in them x2 target blocks x2 calibrated skulk sensors x8 wool blocks water bucket (will need to refill at step 9)  1. Dig 2 blocks below the allay and place a note block  2. To the left/right of the note block (depending on the side of the building you are working on. Left of the building builds left, right builds right) place a comparator pointing into the note block  3. Place another comparator to the  back of the building pointing into the comparator from step 2.  4. Place a lectern to the back of the comparator from step 3.  5. In the lectern, place a book with 15 pages. Flip the book to page 2 of 15. If the comparator from step 3 isn't lighting  up, it is pointing the wrong direction.   6. To the left/right of comparator from step 2, place a target block.  7. To the left of the target block, place a calibrated skulk sensor (cal. sensor) with the purple strip pointing into the target block. Waterlog the cal. sensor to prevent the skulk sensor noises.  8. 1 block above the cal. sensor, Place 4 wool blocks around the cal. sensor to the frond, back, left, and right. There should be an air block above the cal. sensor.  9. Repeat steps 1 to 8 for the opposite side of the building   And that's it. To activate the item fountain, simply walk up to them and jump. Unfortunately,  the allays will continuously drop their items at least 9 times (depending on how many times you jump).  If you have the allays on a lead, place the fence below the note block.
