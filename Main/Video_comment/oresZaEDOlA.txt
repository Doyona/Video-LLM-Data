The Boss has 2 Phases, the beginning one and the phase where he Grafts the Dragon Head onto his Arm. However like other FromSoft Bosses that did the same (Shared Healthbar but a Cutscene for Phase Transition) they will 'die' on screen BUT the Cutscene will still trigger, replace the Boss with the 2nd Phase which then dies again.  Since PointCrow dies during the 1st Phase of Godrick, it prevents the Cutscene from triggering as the game realizes he's dead and it doesn't need to continue to the 2nd Phase. So the 2nd Phase never dies and thus the Fight isn't won.
