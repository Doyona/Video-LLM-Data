If the build is full of visible detail that you want people to be able to see, then make it long If the build is modular with repetitive parts or is something people won't understand on the fly (like redstone builds), then make it short  Short Timelapses serve the purpose of showing the passing of time between scene A and scene B, and all the work involved in it. Long Timelapses serve the purpose of quickly showing HOW you did the build, how it was done in addition to the passing of time and the work.
