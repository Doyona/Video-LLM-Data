The most deaths on road construction equipment is from rollers, where operators try to jump off and get crushed or run over.  Just from the weight alone, and design, there's little control when they start to slide or get knocked out of gear.  You should invest in a ROP and seatbelt to keep the operator in the chair should a rollover occur.  Stay safe young man.
