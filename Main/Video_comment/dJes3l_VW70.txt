Just a note on pocket technique - the reason to use middle and ring finger is not just finger length but also because of how it aligns your wrist, which affects your wrist and finger tendons. If you climb a lot of two finger pockets or pull very hard off pockets, you will be less likely to get soreness or injure due to misalignment if you use the middle/ring technique that Magnus uses. Conversely, you will feel more strain across the hand and through the wrist climbing with the geek climber index/middle technique. You can also feel something similar even more easily in one finger pockets - the middle finger is easier and feels better to hang than the index finger even though many people have a stronger index finger.
