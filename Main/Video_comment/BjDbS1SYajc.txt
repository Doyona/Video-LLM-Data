Protips for newcomers from a crazed obsessor (slight spoilers for surprising moments so if you want to go in blind i suggest you ignore this):   1) You can press and hold B to speed up BASICALLY EVERYTHING. They can be found somewhat frequently on the hidden path/level left of the final boss, on the "snurp" path of course, and they are found in groups of 3 very rare&2 rare or (i think) just 8 rare provided good luck, so its an effective grind spot.   3) Dont bother with RockPaperScissors, i find it much more beneficial to just wait until you get good spinner (or whatever its called) as typically it gives you armour, and you can either equip it or sell it and armour typically goes for MUCH more than 500/1000 gold later on.   4) Fiend enemies only ever take 1 damage from all magic attacks besides bat swarm, so if you have a party of primarily magic users, give them hyper sprinkles if you have them, and also put shield sprinkles on members that are important such as cats and clerics; since their main attack is a one-hit kill.  5) If you're thinking of adding a tank (as in actual tank, it is a class) to your party, i suggest a pop-star as well since Tanks often cause friendships to break; which pop-stars can heal at any time.  6) There's a hidden boss rush in the post-game, and you have to spare the Dark Curse to unlock it.  7) in the first world, dont enter the cave level until most other bosses are done, as the banshee enemies are VERY strong.  8) If you're ever told theres a hidden path, TAKE IT. They often hide high-end foods and armour.  9) In the second world, when asked between the risky and safe paths early on, choose the safe path. The risky path has a piece of armour but it's guarded by two griffins, so dont come back till towards the end of the world  10) In the post-game unlocked island and later districts of NewLumos, dont enter the deep part (or whatever its called) until about level 40, as the enemies there are RIDICULOUSLY STRONG.
