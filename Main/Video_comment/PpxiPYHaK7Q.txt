i want to give some additional tips for catching merchants. There's 4 types of merchants based on the coat they wear. The things they sell do not change regardless of where your base is, their inventory is based on where that particular merchant spawned.  The red coat is a material merchant, he mostly sells crafting materials, and crops/seeds. These guys are good to have a source of something like leather or organs right in your base. High level saddles will need hundreds of leather and i found myself running out every time i wanted to saddle up a legendary pal. They buy anything from your inventory, though some items like keys and eggs are worth zero gold.  The green coat is a gear/ammo merchant, he sells a couple small pieces of gear as well as every type of ammo for guns. The gear is dependent on location of where you found him. so far i have only found one in the volcano's fishing village, and the desert settlement duneshelter. the fishing village guy, being by the volcano, sells fire/heat resistant gear for example. He will also buy anything from your inventory.  The blue coat sells pals. 5 randomly generated ones per day, with the options being based once again on where the merchant spawned. forest pals, volcano pals, desert pals, etc. This merchant will buy ANY pal from your party or box, but will not display the ones from your bases in the sell menu. And yes, ANY pal also means humans. The sell price is based on the rarity of the pal, the level, the passive skills, and whether the pal is alpha or shiny. rarity is kind of nebulous, but if you walk through an area and see packs of 3-6 lamballs, and one mammorest, the mammorest is the rare one. also pals that only appear through boss fights or breeding are extra rare.  the 4th type is the black marketeer, he is huge, scary looking and wears a black hooded cloak. he can be caught, but hes much more difficult than the rest as he uses a gatling gun, and summons his own pal to fight. he offers 5 random pals, seemingly these can be from anywhere, or at least any normal spawns, not sure if he can ever sell boss-only pals like jormuntide anubis jetdragon etc.
