Moreover, a lot of 9-darters occur when one opponent is significantly ahead, not affecting the outcome of a match. In this case, Lipscombe was ahead 3-2 in a first to 4, throwing for the match. Without that 9-darter, Sherrock loses the match, while with it, she ties the score and throws for the match herself.
