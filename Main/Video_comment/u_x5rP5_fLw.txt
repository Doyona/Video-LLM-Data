This is an Automatic Recirculation valve that senses flow drop and automatically recirculates the flow back to tank. Thee "valve" you're asking about to unload the pump is called a by-pass valve. Basically, you want a ball valve linking the pump input to pup output, a pressure gauge on the output will help, then you adjust the ball valve to give an output pressure below the max rating of the pump when your nozzle is closed. We have to use this system on water chillers - the process may stop the flow of water, the pump is dumb and doesn't know that, it just runs, the by-pass allows some water to flow.
