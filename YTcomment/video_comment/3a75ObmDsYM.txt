Use cold water to strain it through a strainer. Remove from the strainer and place it in a bowl to rinse. The water should be â€˜refrigeratorâ€™ cold, not tap water. You can save the buttermilk if you want. I waited until the butter was rinsed to add salt, stirring with a fork. I used Â¼ teaspoon of kosher salt, which has half the sodium of regular table salt.
