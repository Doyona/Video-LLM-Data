Have you ever used a starter strip? You can do it with tubular peyote since you are using the same number of beads on each project. Stitch in all grey a few row of tubular peyote, every 10th bead you string on use a different color (you won't come out even 10th bead),end both ends. With a different color thread than your color for your project. Put on the first row of beads with the different color thread, don't cross threads from the previous row as you put it on (don't tie off). With your main thread for your project, now put on row two, three, etc. When you're done you take off the different color thread. snip it, pull it anything to get it off. Your starter strip will be there for the next project and it was easy to start, hold, and even tension. I didn't read this site thoroughly but basic idea.
