RECIPE FOR THESE CREPES (the crepe only not the filling)  *In a large bowl add:* 2 eggs 1 and 1/2 cups of milk 1 teaspoon of oil, canola oil or vegetable oil (or you can melt 1 tablespoon of butter and pour it in as a substitute) *Blend together well* 1 cup of all purpose flour 1/4 teaspoon of salt *If you want a sweet crepe also add:* 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract 1 tablespoon of granulated sugar *If you would like savory add:* Another 1/2 teaspoon of salt *Blend/Mix together* *You can chill the batter for an hour if you'd like, it is not necessary though.*  *Cooking the crepes* Put some butter in the pan Use 1/4 cup or 1/4 ladle to pour When pouring your batter tilt the pan slightly and lift it off the heat Tilt the pan when the batter is in it so it spreads evenly  Put the pan back on the heat Give it a minute to cook you want the top to be dry Take a spatula after 1 minute and flip it over Let the other side be on the pan for 10-20 seconds  Flip it over and you should see little brown blotches like freckles  Put it on a plate *Repeat until you have no more batter or you can store the batter for up to 2 days*
