I cleaned the commutator with some wet and dry and it started working again.  New brushes are in the post while the machine is in bits.  You can check whether there is a problem with the brushes/commutator without removing the motor by putting a continuity tester across the pins in the big plug (I used an LED and some batteries).  Turn the shaft slowly and there should be no break in the circuit as it turns (the LED stays lit if it is ok).
