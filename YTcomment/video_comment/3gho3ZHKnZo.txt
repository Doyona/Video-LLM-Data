@Mychaela T - Hello! thanks for the kindness. You can certainly modify the pattern to your liking, but because it's an intermediate pattern, it's not very easy to explain in the space YT gives us here. In general if I was to do what you suggest, I would do my right-hand side and center section as the video suggests. Then I would continue by crocheting a rectangular piece equivalent to the size of the right-hand side using double crochet. Then I would go back to the center, reconnect the yarn, and crochet another rectangle, same width, but slightly smaller height, to serve as the pocket. These 2 rectangles should replace the left-hand pockets. Sew the top rectangle to the bottom rectangle as needed. Then I would follow the rest of the pattern as is to crochet the outside cover. :)
