I have tried this product on my damaged white Bekant desk. The filler is a sort of putty or crayon that fills in gouges. There are two white shades to choose from, true white and white. I chose white and it is a reasonable match. But don't expect perfection. This is not going to make your desk look brand new. But if you have gouges in your laminate as I did it will make it look a lot better.  It can be tricky to apply. Sometime you apply some of the crayon stick and as you are smoothing it out, it will all come off revealing the gouge once again. I found it helped use my finger try to smooth the putty without it coming off at times. But I have never done this sort of thing before and my applying of the product was unskillful. A more craft-oriented person would likely do a much better job. My desk was badly damaged and I am happy with the way this has turned out. But as I say, don't expect perfection.
