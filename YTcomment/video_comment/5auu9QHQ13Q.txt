Tip for you if you read your comments. Putting in Faux wood floor dont like up the patterns identically (like you do laying down the next plank at 2:11) it reveals that it is fake wood and doesnt look right. When putting in fake or real wood you stagger the ends so the end lines dont line up by laying down a full length 'board' then a shorter one etc. staggering them. This also prevents the wood pattern repeating in an awkward way. Also, just look because the planks arent all the same. Generally there are like 4 different patterns, so you can alternate them and disguise the fact that they are prints, not real wood....you also do it with the next two planks, putting down two identical pattern ones next to one another, even though they are slightly offset. You did a great job, just for next time know this also...I also see some large cracks over by the bathtub where it looks like you did not get the ends of the planks properly locked together, or perhaps used the wrong ends? and a jagged meeting up with the side of the tub.
