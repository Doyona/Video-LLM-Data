First of all, I do think this is delicious and that is beside the point I wanna make. This is NOT Piccata. I have no idea where the US has the idea that this is Piccata. Piccata is originally a Veal dish, but okay, you call it Chicken Piccata, so that counts as legit. Next is the prep. Till the flour your good. Missing the absolute key ingredient is a sin. You mix eggs with Parmigiano to a thick paste . The floured Chicken goes into that mix and then into your Butter/Oil mix. Fry it indeed to golden brown. Now the sauce. The Milanese sauce is always something with tomato and meat or sausage thingy. Try this: Fully cooked cow thong cut into Julienne, Salami Milano cut into Julienne and half onion rings. Fry in Olive Oil, add some diced canned tomatoes, some tomato puree, fresh chopped basil, salt and pepper. Simmer for a minute or two. Serve the Piccata with Risotto and the sauce.
