In addition to Maya's borax method, ortho fire ant killer, and being tidy, the best thing that worked for me was:  SEAL UP AS MANY ENTRY POINTS AS POSSIBLE. Buy some caulk and use it around fixtures, like ceiling fans, smoke detectors or lights. Concentrate near pipes that carry water, like the kitchen and bathroom: under the sink and behind the toilet where the pipe goes into the wall. Check around windows and doors. If you have cracks in your walls, again use caulk or cover them up with (clear) tape, in corners of the room where the walls meet or behind furniture. Use outlet covers anywhere you don't have things plugged in (you can get them in the baby area of wal-mart or target), because they can come through those too. If you fear them on the bed, try not to have the bed flush to a wall and (this may sound silly) but wrap the feet of your bed with tape, sticky side out (it's catch the babies who try to climb).
