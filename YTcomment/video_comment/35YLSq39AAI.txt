It looks like a tee and at the bottom it has five holes/jets. You can stick it deeper and go around the post. The jets will blast the dirt around the post way better and use up less water.  2. Buy a high lift/off road/farm jack. Put a small chain with a slip hook. Wrap the chain to the pipe and pull up and against the pipe then jack it up till it holds with friction. With the posts you had it would have only taken you 2 min each post and no digging.
