Texas style chili. Cook a mix of hamburger and venison. That gives you a better texture as the hamburger doesn't hold up well to six or more hours of cooking. Add onions, diced or shredded finely, garlic, half a bell pepper. Cook down then add salt, cumin, chili powder. Bloom the chili powder. Add chicken stock and tomato sauce, less acidic than tomatoes. Add red kidney beans. Simmer all day adding water as needed. Before serving mix a couple of tablespoons of corn starch in some water and stir into the chili to thicken it up.
