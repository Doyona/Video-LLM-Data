What helped me in the part where you switch between the index rollover and thumb rollover was this: When you start the index rollover, focus on pulling/throwing the bali towards you, so the momentum pushes it up against your pointer finger, then once you switch to the thumb, focus on pushing the bali away from you so the momentum pushes it towards your thumb. Since you had the knife moving towards you at first you need to switch to pushing away after to maintain that force pushing the bali against your finger so it doesn't fall off. So as you do index rollover think pulling in, and as you start the thumb rollover think pushing out
